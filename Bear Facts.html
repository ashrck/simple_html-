Facts about the Himalayan brown bear

  1. It has been associated with the Yeti
  2. Males range from 1.5 to 2.2 m (4 ft 11 in to 7 ft 3 in) long
  3. They hibernate in dens during the winter
  4. They eat large mammals like sheep

Facts about Grizzly bear

  1. grizzly bears near the coast tend to be larger while inland grizzlies tend to be smaller
  2. A grizzly bear's front claws measure about 51"to "102 mm (2" to "4 in) in length
  3. Around 60,000 wild grizzly bears are located throughout North America
  4. The oldest known wild inland grizzly was about 34 years old in Alaska
